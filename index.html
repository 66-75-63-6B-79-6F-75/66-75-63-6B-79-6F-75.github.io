Scripting Server

"Scripting-Server" is a web-based platform that makes it easy to store and manage your .lua files. It's built using Flask, Ngrok and Python, which helps it run smoothly and efficiently. The platform has a user-friendly interface that lets you easily navigate through your .lua files and even load a specific file with just a click of a button. If you're looking for a convenient way to keep all your scripts organized, "Scripting-Server" has got you covered!
